Sinigang

About the dish:

Traditional and classic Filipino Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 1/2kg pork
  • 1 head garlic
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 pcs bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp whole pepper
  • 1 knorr beef cube
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Saute garlic until brown
  2. Cook pork until it changes color
  3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, water, whole pepper and bay leaves. Let it boil
  4. Add knorr beef cube
  5. Simmer for 30m or until pork is soft enough
  6. Adjust taste with salt
  7. Simmer more unti sauce thickens
  8. Serve hot
